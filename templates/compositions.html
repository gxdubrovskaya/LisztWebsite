The Earlier works of Liszt

The earlier works of Liszt were much more technically challenging than his later works. This was because Liszt wanted to show off his virtuosity. It was rumored that if someone else could play his pieces, he would rewrite them to make it harder to establish himself as the most skilled pianist.
